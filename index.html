##Agenda --- - Feedback? + When to give? + How to give? + How to receive? - 场景分析 - Face To Face Feedback - Discussion

Feedback?

how we are doing in our efforts to reach our goal.

Effective Feedback:

specific information around actions taken to reach a given goal.

Type of Feedbacks

  • Strengthening confidence:

    • + focuses on the positive aspects of one’s behavior.

  • Improving effectiveness:

    • - indicates behaviors to work on and do better.

    When And How To Give Feedback?
    ### When to give? - Earlier - Ask whether you can give feedback - Ask yourself if you should give feedback
    ### How to give? - Make it a conversation - Create a safe environment - Give feedback in private - NO aggression - Not too much
    How to receive feedback?
    ### How to receive - Do not defend - Seek clarifications - Do not forget to say Thank you - Take action
